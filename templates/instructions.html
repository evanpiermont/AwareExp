Thank you for participating!


This is an experiment about decision making. Besides the $0.25 for completing the study, you could get additional payment based partly on your performance, and partly on chance. In the next pages we'll explain (i) the experimental task, (ii) how you make decisions, and (iii) how the extra payment is determined.


Any extra payment will be deposited as a bonus on MTurk within 3 days.

The experiment will use cards containing a certain number (1, 2, 3, or 4) of a particular symbol (triangle, square, circle, or star) of a particular color (purple, orange, red, or blue). Considering all possible combinations of the attributes number, symbol, and color, we end up with 4x4x4=64 unique cards.


As an example, four of those cards are shown below:

From the deck of 64 cards, the computer will randomly select 9 cards. Your objective will be to use those 9 cards to form groups of 3 cards satisfying a specific property, which we'll explain soon. A combination of 3 cards satisfying the property is called a SET.


For today's experiment, you will be paid an additional $0.20 for each SET that you can form.

The property that defines a SET is this: A SET is any combination of 3 cards such that each attribute (number, symbol, and color) is either all the same or all different among the 3 cards.

You will be shown the 9 cards drawn from the deck of 64, and you be given {{rndtime}} seconds to click on the cards to form SETs. The order the cards are clicked does not matter, and you can click a selected card to un-select it.


If you select 3 cards that form a SET, it will be registered on the side bar. If you select 3 cards that do not form a SET, you will incur a 2-second penalty. If you select the same SET more than once, you will also incur a 2-second penalty. Therefore, you should not randomly click on cards in the hopes of finding a SET.

Review and Payment


If you complete the study, you will receive $0.25 for your participation, and an extra $0.20 per SET found. This extra payment will be deposited via a bonus on MTurk and deposited within 3 days.


After the 120 seconds expire, you will be asked to complete a brief survey. After you complete the survey, you will receive your Mturk completion code.
