Task 2


How does it work?


  1. You just received an additional $ 1.00 for passing the comprehension quiz
  2. You must decide how much of this additional $ 1.00 to keep safe and how much to bet in a lottery
  3. You can bet any amount from $ 0.00 to $1.00

How do you earn money?


The amount of money you make will depend on your decision and on chance. There are two possible scenarios:
  • In the GOOD scenario, you earn two and a half times the amount you bet
  • In the BAD scenario, you lose the amount you bet but keep the amount you did not bet

Examples


Suppose you decide to bet $ 0.10 and to keep safe the remaining $ 0.90:
  • In the GOOD scenario you end up with $ 0.10 * 2.5 + $ 0.90 = $1.15
  • And in the BAD scenarion you end up with $ 0.90

Now, suppose you decide to bet $ 0.90 and to keep safe the remaining $ 0.10:
  • In the GOOD scenario you end up with $ 0.90 * 2.5 + $ 0.10 = $2.35
  • And in the BAD scenario you end up with $ 0.10
